 Q: What is the D3FEND matrix?
 A: The D3FEND matrix is a knowledge graph of cybersecurity countermeasures organized into categories and subcategories.

 
 Q: What are some examples of countermeasures in the D3FEND matrix?

 A: Examples of countermeasures include data obfuscation, OS credential dumping, rootkit, process injection, and many more.
 

 Q: What is MITRE D3FEND funded by?
 A: MITRE D3FEND is funded by the National Security Agency (NSA) Cybersecurity Directorate.

 
 Q: Is the use of the MITRE D3FEND website subject to any terms of use?

 A: Yes, the use of the MITRE D3FEND website is subject to the MITRE D3FEND Terms of Use.
 

 Q: What are some trademarks associated with MITRE D3FEND?
 A: Trademarks associated with MITRE D3FEND include the MITRE D3FEND logo and the MITRE D3FEND name.

 
 Q: What is MITRE ATT&CK?

 A: MITRE ATT&CK is a registered trademark of The MITRE Corporation and refers to a knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques.
 

 Q: What is the purpose of the MITRE D3FEND matrix?
 A: The purpose of the MITRE D3FEND matrix is to provide a comprehensive framework of cybersecurity countermeasures to help organizations protect against threats and attacks.
